2024 Stagecoach
Cabernet Sauvignon

High above the valley floor on Atlas Peak, Stagecoach Vineyard is carved from rugged, rock-strewn volcanic soils more than a thousand feet up the eastern hills of Napa Valley. It is one of the most storied hillside sites in California — vines rooted in fractured red stone, drawing on cool mountain air and long, even sun.

Picked at 27.8° Brix and macerated nineteen days on skins, our 2024 is a single-vineyard expression of that place — ripe, deeply structured and built for the cellar. Finished at 14.1% alcohol, it carries the dark fruit and firm, fine-grained tannins of Stagecoach's volcanic terroir.
