2023 Cabernet
Sauvignon

With aromas of blackberry, caramel and spice, and flavors of mocha, plum and cassis, this 2023 Cabernet Sauvignon is a complex wine from the Napa Valley.

Full-bodied with polished tannins and a long finish, delicious now and for the next 20 years. A wine to be shared with those that represent the valley floor as well as the hillside.

96.9% Cabernet Sauvignon  ·  2.1% Petit Verdot
0.75% Malbec  ·  0.25% Cabernet Franc
